Common Brooklyn Flat Roof Issues

  • Brownstone Roof Leaks — Old tar roofs on brownstones deteriorate and leak into top-floor apartments. We remove old layers and install new torch down systems.
  • Parapet Wall Leaks — Brooklyn's row houses have shared parapet walls that are common leak sources. We waterproof and reflash all parapet connections.
  • Ponding Water — Flat roofs with poor drainage accumulate standing water that accelerates membrane failure. We correct drainage and eliminate ponding.
  • Roof Deck Waterproofing — Many Brooklyn buildings have rooftop decks that need proper waterproofing beneath to prevent interior damage.
  • Multi-Layer Buildup — Older buildings often have 3-4 layers of roofing stacked up over decades. We strip to the deck and start fresh.
  • Skylight Leaks — Brooklyn flat roofs often have skylights with aging flashing. We repair or replace skylight connections.

Frequently Asked Questions — Brooklyn

How much does flat roof repair cost in Brooklyn?

Brooklyn flat roof repairs range from $400 for minor patches to $5,000-$8,000 for major section replacement. Full brownstone roof replacement typically costs $6,000-$12,000. Commercial buildings vary based on square footage. We provide free estimates with no obligation.

My brownstone has 3 layers of roofing. Do they all need to come off?

NYC building code allows a maximum of 2 roofing layers. If your brownstone has 3+ layers, they must be stripped to the deck before installing a new system. This actually produces a better result — we can inspect and repair the deck, install proper insulation, and start fresh with a system that'll last 20 years.

Can you repair a flat roof in winter?

Yes, but with some limitations. Torch down roofing can be installed in cold weather (it uses heat). However, some systems like hot-applied coatings work better in warmer temperatures. Emergency repairs can be done year-round. We'll advise you on the best timing for non-emergency work.

I want to build a roof deck. Do I need to waterproof first?

Absolutely. Before any roof deck installation, you need a proper waterproof membrane system underneath. We install the waterproofing layer, then you (or your deck contractor) can build the deck on top. This protects the building below and is required by code.

Do I need DOB permits for roof repair in Brooklyn?

Minor repairs (patching leaks, replacing small sections) don't require permits. Full roof replacements on buildings with 4+ units or over 3 stories typically need a DOB permit and sometimes a licensed engineer's sign-off. We handle all permitting as part of our service.
